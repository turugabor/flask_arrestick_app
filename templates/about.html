About

This app convolutes protein sequences and predicts potential regions with arrestin binding. Further details are available in the manuscript.

The app is written in Python and utilizes sequence and protein structure data from https://alphafold.ebi.ac.uk/.

The project is funded by the Hungarian National Research, Development, and Innovation Fund (NKFI FK 138862).

For more information, please email the author.
